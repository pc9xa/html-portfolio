Patty's Favorite Movies

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Ladybird

A film by Greta Gerwig set in Sacramento, shows a quiet life for a teenager who romanticizes life and wants more.
Eventually she finds that life is not at all what it seems, and finds that life can be found where love is, in any shape, or interpretation.

Soul

Soul is a Disney Pixar animation film that shows what it is truly like to live--that is, that happiness and contentment can be achieved by living in the present, regardless of the situation.

Barbie

Yet another Greta Gerwig favorite, it is set in the imaginary Barbie World; but on a deeper scale, delves into the experiences of female millenials growing up, the expectations we put into ourselves and how, it probably wasn't all that necessary.
